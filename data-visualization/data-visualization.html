This data set describes the amount of noise complaints in Manhattan, NYC in 2016. The size of the dot represents the amount of calls in that location. The bigger the dot, the more amount of calls. I wanted the colors to be vibrant and neon on a black background to give the nightlife feel. Each time the user refreshes the page, they will get a different color combination of dots.
