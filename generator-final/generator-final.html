GENERATOR FINAL

Down arrow:

The motion for this word correlates to the meaning of the word. I used a tall, sans serif typeface too accentuate the up and down motion in the animation. As the user moves their mouse over the word, the bouncing motion will become more extreme.


The word "safe" will be constant on the users mouse, but when they hover towards the center the word, typeface, and color will change. To emulate the police vehical I chose a typeface that is similar to police cars and used flashing red and cyan colors.


As the user moves their mouse across the balloons spelling out "float", the balloons will float up. Once the balloons reach a certain point, it will descend to its original place on the canvas.


When the user goes on the page or refreshes it, the word "surf" will simulate the motion of a wave. I used an bold font that does not have much definition to the letters to make it appear that all the letters are connected. This motion and word emulate a surf board floating on top of a wave.


Each time the user refreshes the page the canvas will start over. The word "cover" will cover the entire canvas in words at random sizes and positions. Also while using this bold font, the opacity is low so that not only is the canvas progressively getting more covered so is the fill color.
